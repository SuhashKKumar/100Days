Returning or Exchanging In-Store Purchases

How to Return In Store

Find a Store Near You

Use our Store Locator to find your nearest store.

Bring the item to the Store

Bring the item in and any one of the following:

  • Receipt
  • Rewards Phone Number
  • Credit Card used for Purchase

Get Refund or Exchange

What Happens Next?

If the refund is applied to your credit card, your credit card company may take up to 10 additional business days to post the refund to your account
